# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

Glow AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information:** First name, age, gender, and ethnicity (optional)
- **Skincare Profile:** Skin type, skin sensitivity, main skincare goals, and skin concerns
- **Contact Information:** Email address for support inquiries

### User Images and Face Data

Our App collects and processes facial images that you provide through:

- **Camera Capture:** Photos taken directly within the App
- **Photo Library:** Images you upload from your device's photo library

**How Your Images Are Used:**

- **AI Skin Analysis:** Your facial images are analyzed using artificial intelligence to assess skin health, identify concerns (acne, dark spots, wrinkles, etc.), and generate a personalized skin health score
- **Face Detection:** We use face detection technology to validate that uploaded images contain a face suitable for analysis
- **Personalized Recommendations:** Your images help us provide customized skincare routines and product recommendations
- **Storage:** Images are stored on our servers and associated with your user account to track your skin health progress over time
- **Local Storage:** Copies of your photos may be stored locally on your device for offline access

**Important:** Your facial images are sent to third-party AI services (OpenAI) for analysis. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

**Why We Store Face Data:**

We store your facial images so that we can display a history of images you have taken in the App, allowing you to view and track your skin health journey over time.

**Face Data Retention Period:**

Your facial images are stored for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all facial images and associated face data are permanently deleted from our servers. ### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- App usage patterns and feature interactions
- Onboarding progress and completion status
- Device information and operating system
- Notification preferences and settings

### Skin Analysis Data

We store the results of your skin analyses, including:

- Skin health scores and breakdowns

- Hydration, elasticity, and texture assessments
- Recommended products and routines
- Analysis timestamps and associated images

### Chat Data

If you use our chat feature, we collect and store your conversations with our AI skincare assistant.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide AI-powered skin analysis and personalized skincare recommendations
- Create and manage your morning and evening skincare routines
- Track your skin health progress over time
- Send you reminders for your skincare routine (with your permission)
- Improve our App and develop new features
- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
- Process subscriptions and payments

## 3. Third-Party Services

We share your information with the following third-party services:

### OpenAI (Face Data Processing)

**Why We Share Face Data:** Your facial images are sent to OpenAI to process your face for skin health analysis. OpenAI's AI models analyze your images to identify skin concerns, assess overall skin health, and generate personalized recommendations.

**Third-Party Storage of Face Data:** OpenAI processes your facial images through their API but does not retain or store your face data after processing is complete. According to OpenAI's [API Data Usage Policy](https://openai.com/policies/api-data-usage-policies), data submitted through the API is not used to train their models and is not stored beyond the immediate processing period. For more information, see OpenAI's [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).

### PostHog (Analytics)
We use PostHog to collect analytics data about how you use the App. This includes events such as onboarding completion, feature usage, and user attributes. You can learn more about PostHog's privacy practices at [PostHog Privacy](https://posthog.com/privacy).

### Superwall (Payments)
We use Superwall to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. Your user ID and basic profile information may be shared with Superwall to process payments and manage your subscription status.

### Convex (Database)
We use Convex as our backend database service to store your user data, skin analyses, routines, and chat history.

### Vercel (Backend Services)
Our backend API is hosted on Vercel, which processes your image uploads and skin analysis requests.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by our third-party service providers
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information
- Local data on your device is stored using standard mobile storage mechanisms
- While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure
